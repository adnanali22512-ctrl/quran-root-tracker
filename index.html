Asal Mafhoom (Root Meaning)

Is root ka asal nishaan kisi aisi cheez ko zahir karna hai jo niche ho, phaili hui ho, ya narm aur thandi ho. Quran mein is se murad hamari Zameen (Earth) hai.

Conceptual Farq (Linguistic Nuance)

Al-Ardh (الأرض) vs Ad-Dunya (الدنيا): Quran mein 'Ardh' physical zameen (crust/planet) ke liye aata hai, jabke 'Dunya' us par mojud aam zindagi ya insani ehsaasaat ke liye istemal hota hai. Both are different concepts!

Scientific & Physics Correlation

Modern geology ke mutabiq zameen ki outer layer (crust) narm aur phaili hui hai jo baki androoni layers ke muqable thandi hai. Quran ka lafz 'Ardh' isi phailao aur narm bichonay (Lithosphere) ki taraf ishara karta hai.
